How it Works

Create and sell NFTs

  • Set up your wallet

    After setting up the wallet of your choice, you click on the "NFT Marketplace" in the top right corner, and connect it to OpenSea. Learn about the wallets we support.

  • Start your collection

    Click on "Create" to set up your collection. You can add social links, a description, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee.

  • Start adding NFTs

    Upload your work (image, video, audio, or 3D art), add a title and description, and customize your NFTs with properties, stats, and unlockable content.

  • List them for sale

    Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your NFTs!
